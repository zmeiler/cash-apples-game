🌱 Product brief

From casual orchard game to scalable mobile engagement platform.

Golden Orchard Pro is positioned as a lightweight mobile web game that can expand into seasonal events, brand sponsorships, classroom/edutainment variants, white-label local business campaigns, and app-store packaging.

Playable coreShake trees, collect apples, earn coins, buy upgrades, complete missions, and progress through tiers.
Monetization hooksCosmetics, premium orchard themes, rewarded boosts, sponsor drops, and seasonal passes.
Enterprise pathAuth, cloud saves, analytics, remote config, admin CMS, A/B testing, and payments can be layered in phases.
DistributionDeployable as a web app, embeddable landing-page demo, or wrapped into Android/iOS later.
RetentionDaily goals, achievement rewards, limited-time crops, leaderboards, and progression pacing.
Investor demoBuilt-in pitch and metrics panels make the product explain itself during the meeting.
Phase 1Polished HTML5/PWA demo, branding, investor story, and live deployment.
Phase 2Backend accounts, cloud saves, analytics events, admin tuning, and device QA.
Phase 3Seasonal content, cosmetics, brand partnerships, and paid boosts.
Phase 4Native wrappers, store listings, school/business editions, and multiplayer competitions.
